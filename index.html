About

Welcome to my creative corner! I’m Abin NR, a flutter developer & web designer with a passion for crafting digital art, currently honing my skills at LBS College of Engineering, Kasaragod. My world revolves around creating clean, intuitive web designs that tell stories and bring ideas to life. When I’m not immersed in code or design, you’ll often find me behind the lens, capturing moments that inspire me. Photography is my way of freezing time and exploring the beauty in everyday details. This website is a reflection of who I am – a fusion of design, creativity, and perspective. Take a moment to explore, and feel free to browse through my captured photographs. I hope my work speaks to you.
